## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Volcano PC safe to use?**  
Use the tool at your own risk. Because it interacts with Roblox processes, it may conflict with the Roblox Terms of Service. Detection-free operation and overall safety are not guaranteed.

**Will Volcano PC continue working after Roblox updates?**  
The updater is designed to address compatibility changes, although support may be delayed following major Roblox patches.

**What makes it different from other executors?**  
Volcano PC emphasizes a lightweight desktop interface, keyless use, and an integrated script hub. Its performance and available features may differ from those of premium alternatives.

**Could using it result in a Roblox account ban?**  
Yes. Any third-party executor can expose an account to suspension. Testing with an alternate account is recommended.

**Where does Volcano PC save scripts?**  
Scripts created or added by the user are kept in `scripts/local/`. Hub scripts are included with the installation.
